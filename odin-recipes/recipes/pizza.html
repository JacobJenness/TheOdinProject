Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Quick, Easy and Delicious-Homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce has never been easier-and this recipe for pepperoni pizza produces a delicious classic! Makes 2, 12-inch pizzas. Total time to make: 30 to 35 minutes.

Ingredients

Contadina Quick Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 (12 oz) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Fleischmann's Easy Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 (.25 oz) envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast or RapidRise Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 (6 oz) package HORMEL Pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste.

  2. Step 2

    For crusts: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar, and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.

  3. Step 3

    Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRise Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)

  4. Step 4

    Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.

  5. Step 5

    For pizzas: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.

  6. Step 6

    Bake for 12 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
