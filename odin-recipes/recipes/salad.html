Bruschetta Salad

Description

This salad tastes just like bruschetta, but in a bowl. If you like bruschetta, you will love this salad! Makes 4 servings. Total time to make: 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma tomatoes, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 pound mozzarella cheese, cut into bite-size cubes
  • 1 cup crushed garlic-flavored bagel chips
  • 1/2 cup torn fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup light olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Combine tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, bagel chips, basil, red onion, olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, basil, salt and black pepper together in a bowl. Toss until evenly mixed.

  2. Step 2

    Refrigerate until chilled, 15 to 30 minutes.
